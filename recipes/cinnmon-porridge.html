Cinnamon Porridge

Description

Have porridge each morning? Why not spruce it up a bit. This porridge recipe will have you kjflsfjsdl.

Ingredients

  • Porridge Oats
  • Semi-skimmed Milk
  • Banana
  • yoghurt

Steps

  1. Put oats, milk and cinnamon in pan, then bring to boil then put on low heat and stir.
  2. Put banana in microwave until soft. Pour porridge into bowl with banana. Dish is served. Bon apetit!
