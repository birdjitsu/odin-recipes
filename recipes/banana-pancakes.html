Banana Pancakes

Description

Love pancakes? Love bananas? Then well, I suppose this is a nice little recipe for you isn't it?

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Baking Powder
  • 2 bananas
  • 2 eggs
  • vanilla extract
  • whole milk
  • butter

Steps

  1. Put flour, baking powder in bowl and smash bananas until smooth
  2. Put butter in pan and wait to melt. Add the batter and cook accordingly
  3. Stack the pancakes on the plate as you finish cooking each one. Apply some syrup or nuts to your taste. Enjoy!
