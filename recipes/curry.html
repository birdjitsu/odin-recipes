Curry

Description

This lovely Indian delicacy is loved all over the globe but is particularly welcomed in our country. A sweet twist on the traditional tika massala, this curry is sure to test your spice threshold!

One bite and you'll be in love.

Ingredients

  • rice
  • chicken
  • spice
  • herbs

Steps

  1. make the rice
  2. cook the chicken
  3. make sure to cut the chicken
  4. prepare the herbs and spices
  5. mix and put in oven for 40 mins
  6. et voila! your dish is ready
